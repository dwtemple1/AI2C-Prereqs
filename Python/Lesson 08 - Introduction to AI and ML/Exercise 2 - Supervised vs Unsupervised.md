# Exercise 2: Supervised vs. Unsupervised Sorting

## Topic: Types of Machine Learning  

Task: For each scenario below, decide if it is Supervised Learning (predicting a label) or Unsupervised Learning (finding patterns).

Spam Filter: You feed the computer thousands of emails labeled "Spam" or "Not Spam" so it can learn to filter your inbox.

Customer Grouping: A grocery store looks at purchase history to find groups of shoppers who buy similar items (e.g., "Health Conscious" vs. "Snack Lovers"), but there are no pre-defined labels.

House Pricing: Predicting the exact sale price of a house based on its square footage and location.

Anomaly Detection: A bank looks for unusual credit card transactions that don't fit the user's normal spending pattern.

(Answers: 1. Supervised, 2. Unsupervised, 3. Supervised, 4. Unsupervised)
